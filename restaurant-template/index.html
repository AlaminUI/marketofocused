Why eat with us

This is such a cool place! converting the old DQ to a hip, multi cultural eatery with ice cream too! we had the noodles and gyro salad bowl. Both were amazing!! Go there support this business!

Kathy Z.

We have been coming to Kitchen Tribes since they opened. They had a few hiccups along the way. But they are responsive to reviews and always strive to make it right for the customer. The food is delicious! Hot, fresh, and the servings are plentiful. The variety and quality keeps us coming back. The owners are very dedicated to success for their business and satisfying their visitors. Try everything once and you’ll definitely find favorites. Ours are the gyros, gyro bowls and both teriyaki offerings. The egg rolls are awesome! And their new noodle bowls are excellent!

Heather Shoemaker

This was the best food in town that we have ever had by a long shot!!! I love how diverse the menus are so we don’t have to compromise on where to go. There’s something for everyone!!! Portions are big enough that most people can make two meals of it depending on what menu you order from. I had the Greek chicken bowl and it was DELICIOUS!!! Definitely will be making this our go to place to eat in town!!!

Sandra Parks

My family enjoyed our time at Kitchen Tribes. There was something for everyone on the menu and it was delicious! The open air seating was perfect for enjoying the great weather and giving my toddler room to move around while we waited for our order. And there is ice cream! We were visiting Labor Day weekend, so many of the downtown South Haven venues were packed. Kitchen Tribes is great for anybody, but especially for people with kids who just need a good, relaxed place to sit and eat.

William Hohnke

Wonderful and expedient service! Signage is great directing food orders to a different location, and ice cream was topnotch. I ordered 2 turtle pecan parfaits. They were made exactly the same. 2 large families came to order during the time I was there waiting and the order taker took their individual orders with exceptions in perfect stride. It was delicious and all customers were happy while I was there!

Kristen Knotek

Kitchen Tribes is so good! We had such a hard time getting into a restaurant anywhere in town and we couldn’t get carry out either. We called five different restaurants that offered carry out and all of them said they weren’t doing carry out that night. We happened to find Kitchen Tribes and the food was delicious and restaurant quality. You could take the food to go or sit there in the picnic area. They had soft drinks available and outdoor games which was great for my young kids.

Jessica Skoryi

We visited for the first time and were quite pleased with what we had. We shared the tropical salad which was incredible and the pot stickers which were as good as any Asian restaurant. We also has the veggie fried rice which was very good, just not crispy enough and was oily but still not enough to warrant a negative review. We were pleased with the amount of food for the price, we were equally as pleased with the ease of ordering and the expediency with which they prepared and served our food.

Kyle McKamey
