Welcome
 
You are among those who understand that government actions to stop the planet’s rising temperature are falling short.
 
The failure of the world’s governments to produce big climate solutions means we all have to work even harder on thousands of smaller ones -- among them, new and better carbon offsets. As a growing number of companies and organizations make net-zero emission pledges, the demand for voluntary carbon offsets has tripled in the last three years and is projected to top 100 billion tons by 2030.
 
We launched Climate Vault to offer a new and better solution for this growing demand, one that provides immediate, verifiable reductions using government-regulated compliance markets and supports innovation in much-needed carbon dioxide removal (CDR) technologies.
 
To date, we’ve helped reduce more than 575,000 metric tons of CO2 on behalf of our supporters by purchasing and “vaulting” carbon allowances. With our newly launched CDR Request for Proposal (RFP), we’re ready to use those allowances to support CDR technologies to make negative emissions possible.
 
As we start a new year, we want to thank you for believing in our outside-the-box approach. We look forward to tackling even more tons in 2022 with you.
 
    - The Climate Vault Team
 
 
 
2021 At-A-Glance for Climate Vault
 
Here are a few of the notable highlights since our launch.
 
• May -- A Wall Street Journal exclusive for our public launch highlighted early supporters Vanderbilt University, TPG, DRW, and more.
 
• June -- Gemini crypto exchange Collaboration to neutralize the negative climate impacts of Bitcoin.
 
• July -- Inaugural meeting of Climate Vault’s Tech Chamber of world-leading science and policy experts to establish the selection, assessment, and measurement criteria for CDR technologies we will support.
 
• August -- NFTs! Heightened interest from the crypto community as NFT artists begin using Climate Vault to neutralize emissions associated with their creations.
 
• September -- American Financial Exchange collaboration to make AMERIBOR® the world’s first carbon-neutral interest rate benchmark.
 
• October -- Selected as one of 15 participants in the Illinois Clean Tech Economy Coalition response to the federal Build Back Better Regional Challenge.
 
• November -- Selected as one of 12 winners (out of 140 applicants) in the Carbon Market Challenge led by the World Economic Forum’s Natural Climate Solutions alliance.
 
• December -- Released our first RFP to provide grant support for 500,000+ tons of carbon dioxide removal from enterprises that meet Tech Chamber approval.
 
 
 
 
What to Expect in 2022
 
2021 was an eventful year full of many successes as we launched the Climate Vault brand and exceeded all of our goals. We’re even more excited about what’s in store for the next 12 months. Here’s what you can expect:
 
• More Tons! We’re only getting started. We vaulted over 575,000 tons in 2021, but we’re aiming for the millions in 2022 and beyond.
 
• Carbon Dioxide Removal (CDR): We’ll be reviewing RFP applications soon. We’ll provide updates as we hope to approve and select CDR enterprises early in 2022.
 
• More Communications & Resources: We’re excited to provide new communications and resources to help you amplify your actions and achievements with Climate Vault.
 
• Enhanced Calculator: We’re enhancing our emissions calculator to help individuals and companies more accurately calculate and offset their emissions.
 
• Team Growth: We’re growing our team to keep pace with increasing demand. Climate Vault is looking for smart, mission-driven people to join our team and make a difference. If you or someone you know is interested, visit Careers.
 
 
 
 
Thank You
 
Thank you for choosing Climate Vault to help you meet your sustainability goals and for supporting our mission. We’ve already made a huge impact together and are ready to do even more together in 2022.
