The median private equity firm spends 80% of its due diligence time on deals that it will not execute. With so many opportunities, it’s crucial to make sure you’re focusing your attention on the best prospects. But how can you do that if you never know what happened to the deals that got away?
 
DealEdge’s new custom  Pipeline Analysis  service allows you to understand exactly that, by comparing the returns of your executed deals to benchmarks of the ones on which you passed or were outbid.
 
Test how successful your pipeline funnel decision-making has been in getting you the best opportunities, and see if you’re underbidding on the deals where you lose out.
 
With Pipeline Analysis, you can:
 
• Discover whether you’re retaining attractive opportunities at the top of your funnel
 
• Test how successful your decision-making has been
 
• See how the deals on which you were outbid performed
