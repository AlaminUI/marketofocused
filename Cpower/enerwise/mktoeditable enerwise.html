Optimize Your
Energy Assets

CPower's EnerWise® Site Optimization solution maximizes the financial return, resiliency and sustainability benefits of energy storage, onsite generation and other distributed energy resources (DERs).

Sustainable

A Customer-Powered Grid will enable clean energy transition investments and counter the impacts of climate change. DER flexibility also supports an organization’s clean energy goals by reducing electricity demand.

Resilient

A customer-powered grid drives community and economic resilience. With our customers, CPower enables the grid to utilize DERs when and where they are needed most to address a growing range of grid needs.

Rewarding

Customers benefit from a customer-powered grid by unlocking the full value of their DERs, which in turn helps them save on energy costs or reinvest in future operational or sustainability initiatives.

The Clean Energy Future is Customer-Powered

Introducing the Customer-Powered Grid™. The grid of the future is balanced, flexible, reliable, and powered by the customers of CPower.

WHAT IS A CUSTOMER-POWERED GRID™?

With 6.3 GW of capacity across more than 17,000 sites in the U.S., CPower rewards energy users for leveraging their DERs to help balance the grid; this creates a Customer-Powered Grid™ that is enabling more-interconnected energy infrastructure to

support community resilience and drive decarbonization. CPower has paid out more than $1 billion in grid revenue to its customers since 2015, and has grown its total monetized MW by 38% over the last three years.
