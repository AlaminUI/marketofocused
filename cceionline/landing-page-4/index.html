CDA® Renewal Amnesty Program

Make your CDA credential active again!

BACK for a Limited Time!

This year marks the 50th anniversary of the CDA®. As part of the celebration, the Council for Professional Recognition is offering a limited-time CDA® Renewal Amnesty Program. This program provides an opportunity for anyone with a CDA expiration date as far back as January 1, 2020, to complete the renewal process and make their credential active again.

Renewal Requirements

Professional Education
Complete 45 clock hours of training in early childhood education/child development within the past three years
Work Experience
Verify 80 hours of professional work experience with young children or families within the past year
First Aid and CPR Certification
Hold a valid First Aid and Infant/Child (Pediatric) CPR certification from a nationally recognized agency
Membership
Provide proof of current membership in a national or local early childhood professional organization
Recommendation
Obtain a recommendation from an early childhood education professional

Application Process:

1. Gather Documentation: Collect all necessary documents, including training certificates, work experience verification, and professional memberships. For more information download the free CDA® Renewal Procedures Guide available on the Council’s website.

2. Complete Training: CCEI’s Got You Covered. We offer comprehensive CDA renewal training programs (< Jump down the page) designed to meet your specific needs

3. Submit Application: Apply online for a renewal fee of $125 or by mail for $150. Applications can be submitted up to six months before your credential expires.
