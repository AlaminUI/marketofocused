Why Our Center-Based Subscriptions?

The Center-Based Annual Subscription is the most manageable, accessible and cost- effective method for a center to provide its staff with training to meet annual licensing professional development needs. CCEI offers two childcare center-based subscriptions, Small and Large available for one full year from the date of enrollment.

  • Unlimited access to over 200 online childcare training courses in English and Spanish
  • Earn 0.1 IACET CEU credit for every one hour of training that you take with CCEI
